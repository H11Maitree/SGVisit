Mahir LABIB

My recent trip to Singapore was a wonderful experience that not only allowed me to explore the city's beauty but also helped me expand my professional network. I met many people from universities, industry professionals, and professors, which was a great learning experience.

I also had the opportunity to bond with new friends from the National University of Singapore (NUS) andthe Singapore Management University(SMU). We explored the city together and tried some of the local delicacies, which broadened my perspective and understanding of different cultures.

The city's transportation system was efficient, clean, and affordable, which made it easy for me to get around and explore different parts of the city. Additionally, the food was diverse, and I enjoyed exploring the different options available, from hawker centers to high-end restaurants.

Lastly, the people of Singapore were warm, friendly, and welcoming, and I learned a lot about their fascinating culture. Overall, it was an unforgettable trip that I will cherish for a long time.

- Mahir LABIB
